Test Your Knowledge of Research Trends

Paper Date Guesser challenges you to guess when research papers were published based only on their abstracts. How well can you identify the era of scientific research?

Play Now View Leaderboard

How to Play

  1. You'll be shown an abstract from a research paper
  2. Read the abstract carefully and look for clues about when it was published
  3. Choose a date
  4. Score points based on how close your guess is to the actual publication date
  5. Play through 7 rounds and compete for the top spot on the leaderboard

Scoring: Error less than 6 months: 100 points, Error between 6 and 36 months: linear decrease from 100 to 0, Error more than 36 months: 0 points
